How to search books by genre?

If you want to search books by genre, simply press the button "Genre" from the navigation bar. Then list of all the genres are shown. When you choose the specific genre you want, list of the books from this genre are shown.

How to contact us?

Simply press the button "Contact us" from the navigation bar. The page contains form from which you can ask your questions or give recommendations about the project.

How to search book?

From the main page, you can search book by "ISBN", "Name of author" or "Name of book". You only have to choose the type of the search from the dropdown next to the search button, and enter the text. Hit the search button and enjoy the results.

What is an ISBN?

An ISBN is an International Standard Book Number. ISBNs were 10 digits in length up to the end of December 2006, but since 1 January 2007 they now always consist of 13 digits. ISBNs are calculated using a specific mathematical formula and include a check digit to validate the number.
